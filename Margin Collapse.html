Margin Collapse

            Top and bottom margins of elements are sometimes collapsed into a single margin that is equal to the largest of the two margins.

            This does not happen on left and right margins! Only top and bottom margins!

            In this example above, the h1 element has a bottom margin of 50px. The pre element has a top margin set to 20px.

            Common Sense would seem to suggest that the vertical margin between the h1 and the pre would be a total of 70px(50px+20px). But due to margin collapse, the actual margin ends up being 50px.
